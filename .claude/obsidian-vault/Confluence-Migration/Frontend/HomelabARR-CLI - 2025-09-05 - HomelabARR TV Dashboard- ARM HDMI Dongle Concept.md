# HomelabARR TV Dashboard: ARM HDMI Dongle Concept

## 🎯 Executive Summary

**Product Vision**: Ultra-compact ARM-powered HDMI dongle that transforms any TV into a HomelabARR OS control center for couch-based homelab management.

**Strategic Positioning**: Fourth product tier in HomelabARR ecosystem - extends Professional Edition to living room entertainment centers.

**Market Opportunity**: $2.3B streaming device market + emerging homelab accessibility needs
## 🏗️ Technical Architecture

### Hardware Specifications (Estimated)

```
ARM-Based Mini Computer:
├── SoC: Raspberry Pi CM4 or RK3588 equivalent
├── RAM: 4GB LPDDR4 (sufficient for Chromium-based interface)
├── Storage: 32GB eMMC (OS + caching)
├── Connectivity: WiFi 6, Bluetooth 5.0, Gigabit Ethernet (optional)
├── Video Output: HDMI 2.1 (4K@60Hz support)
├── Form Factor: Chrome Cast Ultra size (~51mm diameter)
└── Power: USB-C PD (15W max)
```
